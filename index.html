Privacy Policy - Combo Trainer

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Summary: Combo Trainer is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data, do not sell your information, and only use third-party services (Google Play) for payment processing.

1. Introduction

Warren Anderson ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Combo Trainer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

The App collects minimal information automatically:

  • Trial Status: We store the date you first launched the app locally on your device to manage your 14-day free trial.
  • User Preferences: Settings you configure (such as workout preferences, audio settings) are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: Google Play Billing manages your subscription or purchase information. We query Google's servers to verify your premium status but do not store payment details.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or other media
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Device identifiers beyond what's required for Google Play Billing
  • Usage analytics or crash reports (we do not use third-party analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal information we collect is used solely for:

  • Trial Management: Determining if your 14-day free trial is active.
  • Premium Access: Verifying your subscription or lifetime purchase status through Google Play Billing.
  • App Functionality: Storing your workout preferences and settings locally to provide a personalized experience.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Play Billing

For in-app purchases and subscriptions, we use Google Play Billing. Google collects and processes payment information according to their own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing Privacy

4.2 No Other Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage or backup services

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All app data (trial status, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.
  • Data Security: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using a secure lock screen to protect your device.
  • Data Retention: Data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: All data is stored locally on your device and accessible through the app.
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.
  • Manage Subscriptions: Cancel or manage subscriptions through your Google Play Store account.
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the app at any time.

8. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where Google Play operates. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. Since we do not collect personal information beyond what's necessary for app functionality (and stored only on your device), we do not sell personal information. You have the right to request information about data we may have collected, though we collect minimal data as described above.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Since we process minimal data and do not maintain servers, most data processing occurs on your device. Google Play Billing, a third-party service, processes purchase data according to GDPR requirements.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
